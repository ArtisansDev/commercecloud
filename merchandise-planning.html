Benefits of Merchandise & OTB Planning

Flexibility

Open-to-Buy planning empowers retailers to carefully manage inventory levels, striking the right balance between overstock and shortages. Achieving this equilibrium minimizes markdowns and ensures retailers can capitalize on sales opportunities.

Enhanced Cash Flow

By optimizing Open-to-Buy planning, businesses avoid locking up capital in excess inventory. This allows retailers to reallocate financial resources to other vital areas like marketing and business expansion.

Increased Customer Satisfaction

Open-to-Buy planning helps retailers consistently meet customer demand by ensuring products are readily available. This reliability strengthens customer loyalty and promotes repeat purchases, which are critical for growth in today’s competitive retail environment.

Enhanced Decision-Making

OTB provides retailers with a structured framework for inventory planning and purchasing decisions. This allows retailers to focus on products that align with sales targets, allowing for data-driven and informed choices.

Increased Profitability

By optimizing inventory and reducing markdowns, retailers can improve profit margins. Proper use of OTB contributes to more efficient inventory turnover, which ultimately drives profitability.
